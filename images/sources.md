## Recommended Workflow

1. **Search Unsplash first** - CC0, no attribution
2. **Check Pexels second** - Large collection, free use
3. **Try Pixabay third** - CC0, vector options
4. **Use Freepik for mockups** - PSD templates
5. **Generate AI images** - For missing products
6. **Contact brands directly** - For official assets
