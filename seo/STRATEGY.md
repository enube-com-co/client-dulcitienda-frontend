# Dulcitienda - SEO Strategy Document

## Executive Summary

Dulcitienda is a B2B wholesale distributor based in Neiva, Huila, Colombia, specializing in confectionery, snacks, and liquor. This SEO strategy aims to increase organic visibility for wholesale buyers in Neiva and surrounding regions.

### Local Keywords

- "distribuidora mayorista Neiva"
- "confitería mayorista Huila"
- "licores al por mayor Neiva"
- "proveedor de dulces Neiva"
- "distribuidora Neiva centro"
- "mayorista dulces Huila"

### Local Content Strategy

1. **Location Pages:** Create dedicated pages for each secondary city
2. **Local Schema:** Implement LocalBusiness schema with Neiva coordinates
3. **Google Business Profile:** Optimize for local searches
4. **Local Citations:** Ensure NAP consistency across directories

### Regional Considerations

- **Language:** Colombian Spanish with local terminology
- **Currency:** COP (Colombian Peso)
- **Seasonal Events:** 
  - Día de los Dulces (Candy Day)
  - Halloween (growing trend)
  - Christmas season
  - Local festivals (San Pedro, Bambuco Festival)
